ACCOMMODATION

There is a limited availability of accommodation in IIT Patna hostels for student participants at an affordable rate, which will be offered on a first-come-first-served basis. Besides there are several hotels and guest-houses around IIT Patna where the participants may stay during the course.


Note : For transport & accommodation facilities please, intimate us one day before requirement.
